Buzz-In

Campus waste — minimal prototype

Flow (corrected)

  1. Scan item — camera + recognition → app shows which bin to use.
  2. Drop — station sensors confirm disposal (hardware / backend later).
  3. Claim points — you scan the QR shown on the machine. That QR carries drop metadata; the app adds Buzz-Points to your account (stub: local only for now).

Sorting = item scan + guidance. Points = separate machine QR after drop.
